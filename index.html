Human intelligence for confident decision making

Human intelligence is central to our work. We complement our own experience by identifying, engaging, and challenging independent experts who cut through hype and spin to get to the heart of the matter. We harness original thought, deep topic knowledge, and real-world practical experience, combining it with futures thinking to develop actionable insight and succinct recommendations. This human intelligence is often unpublished and otherwise inaccessible.

Partnerships built on intellectual honesty

We form close partnerships with our customers, taking on their challenges as our own, acting as trusted advisors and critical friends. We prioritise intellectual honesty, even when findings are challenging or provocative. Alongside deep research, we help our customers interpret breaking news and emerging trends with rapid conversational insights, clarifying complexity without losing nuance.

A focus on emerging technologies and their public safety implications

While we are generalists at heart, we have extensive experience investigating deepfakes, misinformation, child safety online, and malicious applications of AI, as well as horizon scanning for a wide variety of technologies, harms, and threats. This work informs policy and operations for government departments, regulators, policing, and others.
